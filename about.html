NATIONAL FASHION DESIGNERS SUMMIT 2023

The National Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Fashion Creatives affiliate network in a different region every years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the fashion worlds and knowledge - free fashion advocates, Open knowledge, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different region every years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals.
